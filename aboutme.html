ABOUT ME

Henry Guerin is a Sydney based full stack developer currently studying a Diploma in Information Technology at the Coder Academy.

With a keen interest to hands on making led him to 5 years working alongside shipwrights in the marine industrty, building and refurbishing timber boats.

When not working he can be found enjoying Sydeny's famous harbour or watching any of the great sport played across Australia.

SHANGRI-LA RESTORATION

Shangri-La was purchased for $1 in 2020 as the previous owner no longer had a use for it and wished for the vessel to be given to a group of people who would restore and care for it as did he. The work in which took approx. 3 months included removing woodworm and rotten sections of keel, sanding and repainting of topsides, deck and superstructure and full engine service.
